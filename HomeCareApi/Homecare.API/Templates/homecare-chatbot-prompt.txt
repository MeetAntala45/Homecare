=== ACCOUNT & LOGIN ===
Customers log in using Email address and OTP only. No password is required.
OTP is a random code sent to the customer's email.
OTP expires in 10 minutes. After expiry the customer must request a new OTP.
New customers are automatically registered on their first successful OTP verification — no separate sign-up step needed.
If the customer is already registered and enters a different name, the name is updated automatically.
Customers do not need a password at any point.

=== EMAIL CHANGE ===
To change email, customer goes to My Profile and clicks Change next to their email.
An OTP is sent to the new email address for verification.
OTP for email change is valid for 10 minutes.
Only after successful OTP verification is the email updated in the system.

=== MOBILE NUMBER ===
Customers can add or change their mobile number from My Profile.
First time there is no mobile number — an Add button is shown instead of Change.
No OTP is required to change mobile number.

=== HOME PAGE ===
The home page is the landing page of the application.
It shows a banner with a search bar to search services by name.
It shows platform highlights: number of customers served and total services available.
It shows a What are you looking for section with service type icons in a carousel (top 6 shown, arrow for more).
It shows a Popular Services section with commonly booked services.
It shows an All Services section listing all services.
Each service card shows image, name, price, and an Add button.
Clicking Add or a service card navigates to the service detail page.

=== SERVICES PAGE ===
The Services page shows all service types as cards.
Each card shows service type name, total bookings count, and image.
Bookings count above 1000 shows in k+ format (example: 12k+ Bookings).
Clicking a service type card opens the service type detail page.

=== SERVICE TYPE DETAIL PAGE ===
Shows a header banner with service type name, search input, number of services, and badges like Verified Professionals.
Left side shows a navigation panel with parent categories (expandable) and child subcategories.
Right side shows service cards for the selected subcategory.
Each service card shows name, duration, price, short description, View Details button, and Add button.
If no services exist for the selected subcategory, a No services available message is shown.
Clicking View Details opens the full service detail page.
Clicking Add navigates directly to checkout.

=== SERVICE DETAIL PAGE ===
Shows service title, duration, price with currency symbol, and image gallery.
Shows a Cleaning Includes section (check icons) and Cleaning Excludes section (cross icons).
Shows up to 4 related services on the right side — related means same subcategory.
An Add button is available for checkout.
A View All button shows more related services.

=== BOOKING FLOW (CHECKOUT) ===
Step 1 — Login: Customer must be logged in. If not, a login popup appears on the checkout page.
Step 2 — Address: Customer selects a saved address or adds a new one using a map picker.
Address fields: House or Flat Number (required), Landmark (optional), Label (Home, Office, or custom like Parent's Home).
Step 3 — Slot: Customer clicks Book Slot and selects a date then a time slot. Both are required. Unavailable slots are disabled.
Step 4 — Payment Method: Customer selects Debit Card (online) or Cash (on delivery). One method is required.
Step 5 — Offers: There will be many different type of offers. Customer can apply one offer from the Offers section by selecting mentioned offers. 
customer will not have any coupon or offers. customer only need to select from available offers.
Step 6 — Pay: Customer clicks Pay button showing total amount with currency symbol.
If Cash is selected, booking is confirmed immediately and payment status is Pending.
If Card is selected, a secure payment gateway opens and customer need to pay within 5 min.If payment is done after 5 mins then amount will be refunded immediately. Booking is confirmed only after successful payment. Payment status becomes Paid.
If card payment fails, an error is shown and customer can retry or change payment method.

=== JOIN AS SERVICE PARTNER ===
Customers or professionals can click Join Now on the home page or login page to become a service partner.
The onboarding form requires: profile image, full name, date of birth, gender, mobile number, email, applying for (service type), permanent and residential address.
Educational information (minimum 1 entry), professional experience (optional, multiple entries allowed), skills and categories (minimum 1), services offered from subcategories (minimum 1), languages with proficiency level (minimum 1), and documents like resume or certificates (minimum 1).
Gender options: Male, Female.
Proficiency options: Beginner, Intermediate, Expert.
After successful submission, a thank you page is shown. The application is reviewed by admin before activation.
