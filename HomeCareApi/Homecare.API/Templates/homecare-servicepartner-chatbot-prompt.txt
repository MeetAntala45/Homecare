=== DASHBOARD ===
The dashboard is the first screen service partners see after logging in.
It shows 4 summary metric cards at the top: Total Services Booked, Total Unique Customers, Average Rating, and Total Revenue.
Each card shows the metric value, percentage change compared to the previous week, and a green or red indicator for increase or decrease.
The metric cards do not have any time filter options — they always show overall summary data.
For time-based analysis, use the charts below the metric cards.

Top Performing Services:
This section shows a donut chart with booking distribution across services completed by the service partner.
The center of the chart shows the total bookings count.
Below the chart is a list of services with their individual booking counts.
Time filters available: This Week, This Month, This Year.
Changing the filter refreshes the chart data accordingly.

Revenue Overview:
This section shows a bar chart representing revenue earned per day.
X axis shows days of the week, days of the month, or months of the year depending on the selected filter.
Y axis shows the revenue amount.
Time filters available: Last Week, This Week, This Month, This Year.
Changing the filter updates the chart dynamically.

=== MY JOBS ===
My Jobs page allows service partners to view all their assigned service bookings.
There are two view types: List view and Calendar view. Partners can switch between them.

List View:
The grid shows: Service Name, Booking Date, Slot Time, Customer Name, Address, Amount, and Payment Method.
The grid supports pagination and sorting.
There are two tabs: Upcoming and Completed.
Click Upcoming tab to see future scheduled bookings.
Click Completed tab to see past finished bookings.

Calendar View:
Shows a calendar of the current month.
Each date block displays all services assigned to the partner on that day.
Partners can navigate to previous or next months using the arrow buttons on the top right corner.

=== SERVICES ===
The Services page shows all services related to the service type selected by the partner at the time of registration.
All categories for that service type are shown and can be expanded.
On expanding a category, all subcategories under it are shown.
On clicking a subcategory, all services under that subcategory are listed.
On clicking View Service, the full details of that service are shown including name, price, duration, description, inclusions, and exclusions.
There is an Add button to add a new category or subcategory to the partner's profile.
Partners can select a category and subcategory to offer all services under it.

=== REVIEWS AND RATINGS ===
The Reviews and Ratings page shows the service partner's overall performance based on customer feedback.
Partners can see their average rating displayed prominently.
A rating-wise breakdown shows how many customers gave each star rating (for example: 5 stars : 30 customer, so on).
All individual ratings submitted by customers are listed below.
Each entry shows the star rating given and the written review if the customer added one.
